Why Great Places to School?

“Students spend most of their formative years in schools, and it is critical that schools offer a vital opportunity to ensure that they get the support required to reach their full potential.

What We Do

We are an impact-driven initiative who identify, rate and recognize schools whose innovative learning environment and overall school culture contribute to the wholesome development of our children.

We have bridged the gap by launching a forum that calls for entries from schools in Kenya, verify them within different categories, rank them, and fete them, based on what they offer our students."

Why you should participate

Know what is working best for your School

Assess and benchmark your school culture and be able to identify the gaps and how to best transform your school. Global Peace Foundation resource materials identify the loop holes and provide a roadmap on how to improve – all in metrics that are easy to understand.

Earn National Recognition from Education Stakeholders and the Ministry of Education Character and Creativity Certified Schools have the chance to brand themselves as transformative schools with an inviting school climate by ranking on our prestigious top 100 Best Places to School index annually.

Grow Your Brand as the Best Place to School: Brand yourself as a school that champions virtues of Honesty, Integrity, Respect, Humility, Peace and Unity. Our Certification will give you the marketing resources to build brand recognition and the actionable insights to drive a transformed school culture.
